To be honest there's multiple reasons. For starters I like learning and experimenting with stuff, then after being in the space for over two years I decided to just say fuck it and create a coin on Ethereum Classic because I think it deserves more attention, so I figured what better way to experiment than on the original ethereum chain (which still runs, and always will run on a Proof of Work Consensus Mechanism). Secondly, I'm over the majority of projects going to zero and all of us moving onto the next thing after two days. I've realized if I try to keep up with every single thing in this space I'll go crazy, if I haven't done so already. I'm 31 and didn't know what Computer Science was until I was almost 29. So its fun to learn how to build a website, I enjoy looking into Smart Contracts and trying to make sure everything functions properly, and it's fun to be challenged everyday by people that are pretty damn smart. This is just another part of my journey through the space and I'm enjoying it in the moment with little idea of whats next.
